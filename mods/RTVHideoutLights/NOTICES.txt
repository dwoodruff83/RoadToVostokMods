RTV Hideout Lights — Asset Notices
==================================

This mod ships zero new mesh, texture, or material data of its own. Every
3D model, every material, every texture referenced by the mod's scenes
(.tscn) and item resources (.tres) is loaded from the player's existing
Road to Vostok game install via res:// paths into the vanilla
res://Assets/ tree.

In particular, the mod ships NO copies of any of these vanilla files,
and instead references them at their original res:// locations:

    res://Assets/Candle/Files/MT_Candle.tres

These remain the property of the Road to Vostok developers (Antti Hyytinen
/ Road to Vostok Productions). The mod merely adds new placeable scene
wrappers (.tscn) and item data (.tres) that point at them, plus a small
amount of GDScript to manage placement, switch integration, and on/off
toggle behavior.

Trader catalog icons (mods/RTVHideoutLights/assets/icons/*.png) are
derived from screenshots of the vanilla in-game fixtures, processed for
inventory presentation. These ship inside the .vmz; they are not vanilla
files but are generated from the visual appearance of vanilla content.

If you are the rights holder and want a credit line added or a file
removed, open an issue at
